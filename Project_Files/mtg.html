WEEKLY EVENTS!

Be a part of Sip & Play's community!

We have an abundance of weekly events to relax and find connection.

Our board game cafe aims to facilitate happiness and to bring people together!

Wednesday:

Commander night!

If you love Magic's most popular format, Commander, then this is the night for you.

Every Wednesday at 6pm for just $10 you can play the entire night and join our weekly raffle where we give away playmats, secret lairs or packs to the community.

Our current prizing structure:

2-1: 3 MH3 Play booster packs + promo pack

2-0-1: 5 MH3 Play booster packs + promo pack

3-0: 7 MH3 Play booster packs + foil promo pack

All participants get a free promo and promo pack with us!
